Tips & Home Remedies

Home Remedies for Acne

  • Tea Tree Oil: Dilute with water and apply to affected areas with a cotton swab.
  • Honey & Cinnamon Mask: Mix 2 tbsp honey with 1 tsp cinnamon, apply for 10-15 minutes.
  • Green Tea: Apply cooled green tea to skin or drink 2-3 cups daily.

Recommended Products

  • Salicylic acid cleansers (2%)
  • Benzoyl peroxide spot treatments (2.5-5%)
  • Non-comedogenic moisturizers

Home Remedies for Blackheads

  • Baking Soda: Mix with water to form a paste, gently exfoliate.
  • Steam Treatment: Steam face for 5-10 minutes to open pores.
  • Clay Masks: Use bentonite or kaolin clay 1-2 times weekly.

Recommended Products

  • BHAs (salicylic acid) 1-2%
  • Charcoal-based cleansers
  • Retinoids (for prevention)

Home Remedies for Dark Spots

  • Lemon Juice: Apply diluted lemon juice to spots (avoid sun after).
  • Aloe Vera: Apply fresh aloe gel overnight.
  • Vitamin E Oil: Massage into spots daily.

Recommended Products

  • Vitamin C serums (10-20%)
  • Niacinamide treatments (5%)
  • Chemical exfoliants (AHAs like glycolic acid)

Home Remedies for Wrinkles

  • Coconut Oil: Massage into skin at night.
  • Egg White Mask: Apply whipped egg whites for temporary tightening.
  • Avocado Mask: Mash avocado with honey, apply for 15 minutes.

Recommended Products

  • Retinol creams (start with 0.25-0.5%)
  • Peptide serums
  • Hyaluronic acid moisturizers
